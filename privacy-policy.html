RoastLord Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

RoastLord ("we", "the app") is an entertainment app that generates humorous roasts from photos you upload. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Photos & Content

  • Photos you upload are processed to generate roasts.
  • Photos are not stored on our servers. Processing happens via the xAI API when you provide your own API key, or locally with demo content when no key is set.
  • Photos remain on your device unless you choose to share or save roast cards.

API Keys

If you use real AI roasts, your xAI API key is stored only in your device's local storage. It is sent directly from your device to xAI's servers. We do not collect or store your API key on any RoastLord backend.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration required
  • No sale of personal data
  • No tracking across other apps or websites
  • No persistent storage of your photos on RoastLord servers

Third-Party Services

When you enable real AI roasts, your photo and prompt are sent to xAI per their privacy policy. Font and UI assets may load from Google Fonts and Tailwind CDN when online.

Children

RoastLord is intended for users aged 13 and older (17+ recommended due to mature humor). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@roastlord.app
